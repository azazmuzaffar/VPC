ROYAL FOAM FACTORY

Royal Corporation foam production facilities come fully equipped with top-of-the-line high-pressure continuous polyurethane foam machines, churning out regular and finer cell structure polyurethane foam with uniform hardness and density distribution consistently and rapidly — More foam for less time.

International standards have come to be defined by this technology, and Royal is at the forefront of pioneering more efficient processing solutions for highly-qualified products. To streamline a robust production process, Royal's plants implement a flow that minimises clutter and maximises efficiency.
